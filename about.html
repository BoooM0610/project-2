About Us

Origin

Established in 2019, this business was created with the aim of making brushing your teeth a fun activity. Our programme is focused on kids between the age of 4 to 12. As oral hygiene is crucial to the development of the teeth, specially at an early age.

Targets

  1. Try to keep children amused while brushing their teeth.
  2. Try to make our toothbrush the most valued among children's toothbrushes.
  3. Trying to improve children's hygiene to give them the best smile.
